Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shelf life of your products?

Our products have a shelf life of 6 months from the date of manufacturing when stored in a cool, dry place.

No, we do not use any artificial preservatives. Our namkeen is made fresh with natural ingredients and traditional methods.

Do you deliver across India?

Yes! We deliver to all states across India. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days depending on your location.

Are your products vegetarian?

Yes, all our products are 100% vegetarian and made with pure vegetarian ingredients.

Can I order in bulk for events?

Absolutely! We offer special bulk pricing for weddings, corporate events, and festivals. Contact us for details.
